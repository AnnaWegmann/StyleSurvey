This dataset was also compiled with resources from [Huang et al., 2025, Authorship Attribution in the Era of LLMs: Problems, Methodologies, and Challenges](https://doi.org/10.1145/3715073.3715076). Authorship Attribution datasets can usually also be used for authorship verification.

Note that several commonly used datasets in the 2010s and before might not be accessible anymore, or not recommended to use because of ToS and copyright conflicts. Proceed with caution. We try and highlight safe corpora (✓), but give no guarantees.
