We collect datasets that have been used for style-related tasks before. These datasets were also compiled with resources from [Huang et al., 2025, Authorship Attribution in the Era of LLMs: Problems, Methodologies, and Challenges](https://doi.org/10.1145/3715073.3715076). (Since they provide many machine text detection resources and that task was not our focus, we did not repeat them here.) Authorship Attribution datasets can usually also be used for authorship verification. Note that many more datasets could be used for style-related tasks.

Note that several commonly used datasets in the 2010s and before might not be accessible anymore, or not recommended to use because of ToS and copyright conflicts. Proceed with caution. We try and highlight safe corpora (✓), but give no guarantees.

*We welcome additions via Pull Rrequests!*
